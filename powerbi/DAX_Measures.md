# DAX Measures

## Overview

This document outlines the DAX measures created in Microsoft Power BI to support KPI calculations and interactive dashboard reporting for the CareBridge Health Network Analytics project.

These measures were used across the Executive, Operational, and Analytical dashboards to monitor financial performance, operational efficiency, and patient statistics.

---

# Revenue Measures

## Total Revenue

Calculates the total revenue generated by the hospital network.

**Purpose:** Executive KPI and revenue analysis.

---

## Paid Revenue

Calculates the total value of bills with a payment status of **Paid**.

**Purpose:** Tracks successfully collected revenue.

---

## Pending Revenue

Calculates the total value of bills that remain unpaid.

**Purpose:** Monitors outstanding revenue awaiting payment.

---

## Collection Rate

Calculates the percentage of billed revenue that has been successfully collected.

**Purpose:** Measures payment collection efficiency.

---

## Average Bill

Calculates the average billing amount per patient.

**Purpose:** Evaluates the average revenue generated from each billing record.

---

# Appointment Measures

## Total Appointments

Counts all appointments recorded during the analysis period.

**Purpose:** Executive KPI and appointment analysis.

---

## Completed Appointments

Counts appointments with a status of **Completed**.

**Purpose:** Measures successfully completed patient visits.

---

## Cancelled Appointments

Counts appointments with a status of **Cancelled**.

**Purpose:** Monitors appointment cancellations.

---

## Completion Rate

Calculates the percentage of appointments that were completed.

**Purpose:** Measures operational efficiency and appointment success.

---

# Patient Measures

## Total Patients

Counts the total number of registered patients.

**Purpose:** Executive KPI.

---

## Male Patients

Counts the number of male patients.

**Purpose:** Supports gender distribution analysis.

---

## Female Patients

Counts the number of female patients.

**Purpose:** Supports gender distribution analysis.

---

# Clinical Measures

## Total Doctors

Counts the total number of doctors within the hospital network.

**Purpose:** Executive KPI.

---

## Treatment Count

Counts the total number of treatments performed.

**Purpose:** Measures treatment activity.

---

## Average Treatment Cost

Calculates the average cost of treatments.

**Purpose:** Evaluates the average cost of healthcare services provided.

---

# Dashboard Usage

These DAX measures power the KPIs and visualizations across the three Power BI dashboards:

- **Executive Dashboard** – High-level organizational performance.
- **Operational Dashboard** – Financial and operational monitoring.
- **Analytical Dashboard** – Patient and doctor analytics.

Together, these measures provide stakeholders with reliable metrics for monitoring hospital performance and supporting data-driven decision-making.
